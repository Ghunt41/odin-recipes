Fettuccine Alfredo

Ever wanted the key to forver happiness? Well you've stumbled onto the right webpage. Beware, this Fetticcine Alfredo reciple can and will change your life.

Ingredients

  • 24 oz Fettuccine Pasta
  • 1 cup of Butter
  • 3/4 pint of Heavy Cream
  • Salt, Pepper, and Garlic Salt to taste
  • 3/4 cup Romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot to boil, add in your pasta and cook for ~ 10 minutes, or until you like it. Then drain and set aside.
  2. In a large saucepan, melt the butter and the cream together over low heat. Add the seasonings in as well.
  3. Increase the heat to medium and stir in the cheeses until it has all melted together and thickened.
  4. Add the cooked pasta into the mixture and stir until satisfyed.
  5. Serve up your masterpiece and change lives!
