No-Bake Cookies

These No-Bake cookies will not only melt away your taste buds, but all of your life's problems too. You do not want to pass these up!

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups White Sugar
  • 1/2 cup of Butter
  • 1/2 cup of Milk
  • 4 tablespoons Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • 1/2 cup Crunchy Peanut Butter
  • 3 cups of Quick-Cooking Oats
  • 1 teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  • Some Wax Paper

Steps

  1. Get your Sugar, Butter, Milk, and Cocoa mixed together in an average saucepan. Stir it often until it boils. Let it boil for 1 1/2 minutes.
  2. Right after the 1 1/2 minutes boiling, remove it from the heat and stir in your Peanut Butter.
  3. Right after the Peanut Butter, stir in the Oats and the Vanilla as well. Mix it all thouroughly.
  4. Use whatever you'd like to seperate the mixture into the cookie sizes that you'd want on the Wax Paper.
  5. Let the cookies sit until they are hardened and enjoy a stress-free life. Until they're all gone that is.
